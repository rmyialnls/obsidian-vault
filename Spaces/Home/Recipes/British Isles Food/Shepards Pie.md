# Ingredients

- 2 tbsp olive oil 
- 2 lbs ground lamb, beef, pork or a mix 
- 1 large onion, grated 
- 1 large carrot, grated
- 2 cloves garlic, grated
- 2 tbsp worcestershire sauce 
- 1½ tbsp tomato paste 
  2 Tbsp siracha
- 2 sprigs of fresh thyme leaves 
- 1 sprig fresh rosemary, chopped 
- 1 cup red wine 
- 1 cup chicken stock
- 1 cup of peas
## [[Mashed Potatoes ]]
- 3 lbs potatoes (peeled and cut into chunks) 
- 3 egg yolks 
- 1 cup milk
- 5 tbsp butter
- 7 tbsp parmesan cheese, grated
- 1 cup old cheddar 
- 1 handful of fresh chopped chives
